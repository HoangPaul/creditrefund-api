Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1st May 2016

What information we collect?

In using our "Credit Refund" Android application, you will be asked to provide the following information in order to process your order:

  • Email address

You may also need to provide the following information should you choose to receive your payout via bank transfer:

  • Bank account name
  • Bank account number
  • Bank-State-Branch (BSB) number

When do we collect your information?

We collect information you provide when you perform the following actions in our app:

  • Calculate your payout amount
  • Place your order

Why do we collect your information?

The personal information you provide is used to send money to your nominated account via a payment system. The payment systems we use to send money are as follows:

  • Paypal
  • Pin Payments (used for bank transfers)

In addition, we will use also your information to perform the following operations:

  • To help us assist your customer support queries.
  • To contact you if there has been an error in processing your order.

Security

All personal information are encrypted in transit using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol and is stored on a secure network which is behind a enterprise grade firewall and only accessible to authorised users.

Third parties

We do not sell, trade or otherwise share any personal identifiable information to third parties. This does not include payment systems, as they require your information to send money to your nominated account.

Cookies

We do not use cookies on this website.
