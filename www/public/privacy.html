Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1st May 2016

What information we collect?

In using our "Credit Refund" Android application, you will be asked to provide the following information:

  • Email address

You may also need to provide the following information should you choose to receive your payout via bank transfer:

  • Bank account name
  • Bank account number
  • Bank-State-Branch (BSB) number

When do we collect your information?

We collect information you provide when you perform the following actions in our app:

  • Calculate your payout amount
  • Place your order

Why do we collect your information?

The personal information is used to send money to your nominated account via a payment system. The payment systems we use are as follows:

  • Paypal
  • Pin Payments (used for bank transfers)

In addition, we will use also your information to perform the following operations:

  • To assist in your customer support queries.
  • To contact you if there has been an error in processing your order.

Security

All personal information are encrypted in transit using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol and are stored on a secure network.

Third parties

We do not sell, trade or otherwise share any personal identifiable information to third parties. However, in order to send funds to your account, we have to share payment information to the payment system.
