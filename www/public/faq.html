Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for the funds to appear in my account?

It depends on which payment method you choose. PayPal will usually deposit the funds in your account within a few hours. Bank transfers takes a few days to clear for the funds to appear in your account. In either case, it should take no longer than 3 business days.

What happens if there's something wrong with my order?

If we cannot send the funds to your account (for example, if your details are invalid), we will contact you via email to arrange an alternative payment method.

What is the minimum amount I can convert?

Any amount, as long as the amount you receive is greater than $0.01.

What is the maximum amount I can convert?

Currently, the maximum amount you can convert is $100 per transaction.
