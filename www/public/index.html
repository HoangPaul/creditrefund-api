Our fee structure

We are transparent on the fees involved in our service. The following is a breakdown on the fees that will affect your payout:

30% Google Play services
Google takes 30% for all in-app purchases
~2% payment services
Payment services charge a fee to transfer money to another account.
A list of payment services and their fees are as follows:
  • Paypal: 2%
  • Pin (bank transfer): 2% + $0.30
12% processing fee
We charge a fee for all orders, most which goes into paying for development and server maintenance.
